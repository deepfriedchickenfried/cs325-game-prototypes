Q F J P

  • Originality: It is a somewhat unique physics based multiplayer concept that only uses 1 button.
  • Technical Merit: This game uses Phaser's p2 physics instead of its Arcade physics and makes full use of collision groups. One unique thing about this game is that the majority of the players movement is done through collisions
  • Prototype Postmortem: I learned how to use p2 physics and that bodies made using arcade physics can't rotate. The easiest part of making this game was the text and camera flash at the end of a round. The hardest part of making this game was figuring out how to use the p2 physics.
  • Prototype Assets: Virtually all of my assets are made from scratch except for the music.
    • Out of Time by Abstration from the album Three red hearts prepare to dev: Three red hearts
  • Prototype Closest Other Game: Can't really think of a similar game. I guess it would be close to other top down multiplayer 1 button games. This time I didn't actually take any code from other games.
  • High Concept: A fast pace 4 person multiplayer game where each must control a spinning character and try to stab the others.
  • Theme: None
  • Mandated Variety: This is a multiplayer game.
  • Prototype Goal: This prototype is evaluating the movement of characters and the overall feel of the game.
  • Player Experience Goals: I simply want players to have fun and feel a little challenged despite the fact that there is a good amount of randomness.
  • Gameplay: This game is a 4 player game with only 4 buttons in total. The red player is controlled with Q. The blue player is controlled with F. The green player is controlled with J and the yellow player is controlled with P. When the button is not pressed each player will swing their sword around them very quickly and pressing the button will cause the player to stop rotating and to move forward in their current direction. If a players sword hits a wall or another sword while rotating they will most likely be propelled in a certain direction. Players are less likely to be propelled if their button is pressed. Each player has three lives and can lose a life if their body is hit by an opponents sword. When a player loses a life if they have any remaining lives they will be sent back to their original spawn. One future feature for this game would be some way to prevent spawn camping like the spawn being accessible only to its own player or giving the player invinsibility frames. Additional features could be tiles that grant abilities when walked on like more swords, a different weapon or a speed boost. The main goal of the game is to be the last man standing at which point the screen will flash in the winners color and the game will reset.
  • Strategies: Some strategies that I think will be useful is to not hold down the buttons for too long as it can leave players open to attack. Since the players don't stop immedietly when the button is released another strategy is to charge towards someone and release the button early so that the player will begin to spin as he slides to his opponent.
  • Story/Setting/Premise: Four people who aren't really good at sword fighting will swing their swords around till the end of time to some sweet tunes. In all honesty though, there isn't much story here.
  • Target Audience: I am trying to reach casual gamers that like to have fun with their friends without needing a bunch of controllers.
  • Play Time: Takes around 5 minutes to play.
