Vomit Comet version 2.0

  • Originality: Top down puzzle game where, you are a parasitic organism that can explosively leave its host in order to infect another and must use that power to escape a lab before scientists can perform terrible experiments on you.

  • High Concept: You are a parasitic organism that must leave a lab and can launch out of people into others to survive.
  • Prototype Goal: This prototype is continuing to test out the movement and mechanics as well as the newer objects.
  • Player Experience Goals: Want players to have fun as they try to solve each puzzle
  • Gameplay: The player can move with up down left right. You start at the green person and can press in any direction and your goal is to reach the orange person. When you are in a host you can leave the host and move in any direction. When you are in the air, you have one projectile charge and can use that projectile charge to change your direction by pressing a key. Changing your direction this way will launch a projectile in the opposite direction. The projectile can be used to kill enemies that block your path and eventually trigger buttons. When you collide with a person your charge is refreshed. The game ends when the player collides with a red enemy or wall or if the orange enemy dies by a projectile. There are also several obstacles that the player can interact with. The corner pieces will bounce the player or projectile if it is going a certain direction and will do nothing otherwise. The mini blocks allow projectiles to pass through it but not players and the one way blocks only allow objects through it that are going in a certain direction. Finally there are destructible blocks that act like normal blocks except for the fact that they can only sustain one hit. There are several different types of enemies. The red enemies kill you if you touch them, but can be killed by the projectile. The black enemies are normal and can be infected or killed with a projectile The white enemies are light enemies that move one space when infected The clear enemies are ghost enemies that disapear immediatly when infected
  • Strategies: The main strategy is to carefully plan out your intended moves before you launch yourself.
  • Story/Setting/Premise: The premise of the game is that you are an intelligent parasite that was captured by scientists. They would constantly perform terrible experiments on you and so you have had enough and want to get revenge and escape. Eventually as they continue to perform experiments you learn that you can infect living organisms and then launch yourself from them. This does leave the hosts though very dead. You are very fragile though so if you get hit more than once when you are outside of the host you are dead for good. Luckily for you the scientists have stored a large amount of you and so you can try to escape for as long as you want.
  • Target Audience: People that like top down puzzle games
  • Play Time: About 5 minutes
