Aerial MayDay!

  • Originality: This is an iteration on my previous game with the addition of weapons
  • Technical Merit: I added both a forward firing gun and a bomb
  • Prototype Postmortem: It was hard to implement the bomb to my liking and even now it doenst always work;
  • Prototype Assets: Did you make your prototype assets from scratch? Did you borrow them? Cite your sources here. Everything is made from scratch except for the loading screen and the jazz music
  • Prototype Closest Other Game: Aerial Assault
  • High Concept: Dodge an increasing number of missiles to escape enemy territory
  • Theme: Escape
  • Mandated Variety: Added weapons
  • Prototype Goal: Interating on a previous game
  • Player Experience Goals: Have a fun and stressful time dodging missiles and destroying the ones you cant
  • Gameplay: Dodge the increasing ammount of missiles until time runs out. Use the LEFT and RIGHT arrow keys the turn left and right and UP to accelerate You can also press x to shoot and c to drop a bomb. You can also press z to spin your plane but it doesnt do anything
  • Strategies: Utilize going off the screen as much as possible. While disorienting, you can't be blown up off screen. Shoot the ones that are about to hit you
  • Story/Setting/Premise: Trying to escape in a small fighter plane
  • Target Audience: Everyone.
  • Play Time: 1-5 minutes
