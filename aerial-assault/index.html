Escape Enemy Terroritory!

  • Originality: I added a few new concepts to one of my favorite games I saw in class so far
  • Technical Merit: I borrowed the code from the original game, but I did implement what I thought would be fun additions to this game
  • Prototype Postmortem: Playing a game and then thinking fun things to add to it is a very effective way of prototyping

    • Original game: Aerial MayDay
    • Main Menu Picture: here
  • Prototype Closest Other Game: Aerial MayDay
  • High Concept: Dodge an increasing number of missiles to escape the enemy country
  • Theme: Escape
  • Mandated Variety: Random Missile locations
  • Prototype Goal: Interating on a previous game
  • Player Experience Goals: Suffer through strategizing how to dodge all the missiles
  • Gameplay: Dodge the increasing ammount of missiles until time runs out. Use the LEFT and RIGHT arrow keys the turn left and right and UP to accelerate
  • Strategies: Utilize going off the screen as much as possible. While disorienting, you can't be blown up off screen.
  • Story/Setting/Premise: You are a scholar of science and are trying to flee the enemy country after being captured.
  • Target Audience: Everyone.
  • Play Time: 1-5 minutes
